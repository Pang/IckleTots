About Us

Ofsted registered, inspected and approved, fully qualified (to NVQ level 3) Childminder since the 30th June 2008. Home-based child care provided for ages from birth to 19 years of age, including 2-4 year old funded placements.

Trained in Paediatric First Aid as well as having received the highest grade as a food business with the local authority environmental health.

I am committed to creating a safe, secure and fun environment for the children under my care; whilst being mindful and flexible to the needs of the parents who require my service.

My provision has a ground floor entrance, with a ramp and double doors providing easy access for double prams, pushchairs or wheelchairs. Safe off road parking is accessible on the driveway or on the facing layby.

A secure outdoor play area. Where physical activities in a safe environment with age/ size appropriate play equipment and resources are provided. Situated close to local schools and facilities, such as the library, parks, and sure start center. With permission from parents to take their children on routine outings and trips by foot or by car.

My vehicle is insured for business use and fitted with suitable age/size appropriate car chairs and booster seats. Written risk assessments, policies and procedures are in place for trips outings. Healthy and nutritional meals and snacks are provided. Following the Early Years code of practice for food and drink, and the food and drink guidelines for early year's settings. I work together with families and obtain information prior to children attending the setting regarding any special dietary requirements, food allergies, intolerances, cultures, religions and beliefs. Ensuring that all children attending the setting enjoy appropriate meals and snacks.

Fees

~Full Time Places~
Monday to Friday 8:00am - 17:00pm: £150 per week/£30 per day.

~Part Time Places~
Hourly rates and part time sessions are charged at £4 per hour.

~Wrap Around Care~
Drop off at local schools 7:00am - 9:00am: £10 per day. Collections from local schools 15:00pm - 17:30pm: £10 per day.

Cheaper rates are available for additional siblings attending at the same time as the youngest child.

An additional fee of £4 will be added to the Childcare fees for any out of contracted hours and some late collections (please see my uncollected children policy).
